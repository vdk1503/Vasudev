// 01 — about

Where deep process science meets real-time data.

I'm an accomplished Chemical Engineer and R&D specialist with over 20 years in Process Engineering — specializing in Pre-FEED, FEED, and detailed engineering for onshore and offshore projects. My core expertise spans OLGA, LedaFlow, K-Spice, and ASPEN HYSYS dynamic simulation, real-time monitoring, and production optimization.

Across Falkor (Kongsberg Digital), Shell, ABB, P&G, and Invensys, I've delivered multimillion-dollar impact for upstream assets and FPSOs — from a global enterprise simulation rollout at Shell across 18 sites to real-time production systems for ONGC and Reliance. Lately I've been building GenAI and LLM-powered tools that put decades of process know-how directly into engineers' hands.

I hold a PhD from IIT Madras and a postdoctoral fellowship from the Max Planck Institute, with 7 publications and 3 patent filings. I'm looking for a senior role where process excellence and AI drive the business — and I'm open to relocate.
